## Quick Method

1. **Open teecherz.com**
2. **Right-click on any colored element** → Inspect
3. **In DevTools, look at the Styles panel** → Find the `color` or `background-color` property
4. **Copy the HEX code** (e.g., `#5c62e4`)
5. **Fill in the template above**
